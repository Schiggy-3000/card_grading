### Features
1. Card recognition
- Only cards from the card games Magic the gathering and Flesh and blood are in the scope
- The card recognition feature recognizes a given card by a picture of its frontside
- Not only the correct card, but also the correct version (edition, promo, foil, foil type, language, collectors edition, ...) must be identified
- A confidence score that represent the certainty with which a card was recognized is also shown
- A picture of the identified card is fetched (e.g. from an API) and also shown. This lets the user quickly verify that the card recognition feature recognized the correct card. It is vital that not only the correct card but also the correct version (edition, promo, foil, foil type, language, collectors edition, ...) of the card is displayed.
- A graph that shows the avg. selling price must also be shown. The x-axis shows the time, the y-axis the avg. price. The ranges for both axes should not be static but be adjusted to the available data points

2. Card grading
- Only cards from the card games Magic the gathering and Flesh and blood are in the scope
- The card grading feature grades a given card according to the selected grading standard (PSA, BGS, CGC, TAG) by a picture of its front and backside
- A grade estimate for the selected grading standard is shown
- A detailed description for why a given grade came to be must be shown
- The card grading feature must also recognize the card (correct card and correct version) and show its details (name, version, etc.)
